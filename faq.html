FAQs

What is the purpose of this dashboard?

The dashboard provides real-time information about air quality, helping users monitor environmental conditions and make informed decisions based on current data.

Where does the data come from?

Data is sourced from reliable agencies and open APIs, ensuring accurate and timely information on air quality levels.

What pollutants are monitored by this dashboard?

The dashboard tracks various air quality indicators, including PM2.5, PM10, NOx, CO2, SO2, VOC, and more.

How often is the data updated?

The data on the dashboard is updated in real-time, allowing users to stay informed of any significant changes in air quality.

Is this dashboard accessible on mobile devices?

Yes, the dashboard is designed to be responsive and accessible on both mobile and desktop devices.
